Redesign of University Grants Commission website

The redesign of the University Grants Commission (UGC) website aimed to enhance accessibility and improve user engagement for students, educators, and stakeholders. The new design features a clean, modern layout with intuitive navigation, making it easier for users to access essential information about higher education in Sri Lanka. Key functionalities include a responsive design, an interactive news section, and comprehensive resources for universities and students. By implementing user feedback and best practices in web design, the redesign not only elevates the UGC's online presence but also fosters transparency and communication within the education sector.
